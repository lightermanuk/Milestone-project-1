Tourist Attractions

The limestone Island of Portland is located in South Dorset, and is connected to the mainland via Chesil beach, a pebble beach stretching for 18 miles along the Dorset coast.

Portland Bill Lighthouse

Standing sentinel at the island's southern tip, this iconic lighthouse boasts breathtaking panoramic views. Explore the visitor center and delve into the rich history of maritime navigation. Fun fact: the lighthouse was once nicknamed "the island's fiery finger" for its role in guiding ships through treacherous waters.
