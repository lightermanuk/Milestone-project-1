Walks

Fancy seeing Portland in 3D and in 4K so you can get your bearings? Check out these vids!!

Portland is walkers paradise! It has unique challenges geographically and weather wise also. Here are 3 of the top walks on the Island.

Southwell to Portland Bill: (Easy Difficulty)

Verne High Angle Battery Walk: (Medium Difficulty) Delve into Portland's military past with the Verne High Angle Battery Walk. Explore the network of tunnels and gun emplacements built to defend the island from invasion. This walk offers fascinating insights into Portland's strategic importance and provides stunning views of the surrounding coastline.

Portland Circular Walk: (Hard Difficulty) Discover the hidden gems and rich history of Portland on this circular walk. Explore the charming streets of the old town, visit the impressive Portland Castle, and take in panoramic views from the heights. This walk is a perfect way to get a well-rounded experience of this captivating island.
